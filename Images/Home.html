Welcome to the official website of Australian Council For Clicnical Physiologists

The Australian Council for Clicnical Physiologists (ACCP) has been established with the remit of initiating a national planto implement voluntary accrediation for Australian Clicnical Physiologists (i.e. Clinical Measurement Scientists)

Key to this process is the protection of the general public through the accountability that accrediation affords, with in each discipline's scope of practice, for those professionals employedin public and private hospitals and clinics throughout Australia.

The ACCP is a voluntary register for health professionals working in the desciplines of Clicnical Physiology including:

Cardiac Science Clinical Neurophysiology

Respiratory Science Sleep Science
